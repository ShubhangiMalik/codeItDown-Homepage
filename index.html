About Us

codeItDown is a technical community where we provide tech courses with a motive of learning together.

We believe that learning becomes more pleasurable when it will be a two-way communication. We arrange live sessions and burn the lamp of wisdom and knowledge.

Our sessions are very interactive and the trainers have rich experiences.

codeItDown allows the students to participate in an open discussion.

We don't have any hierarchy. Our team works together and believes to grow together.

Our working culture is pretty good with the intension to share knowledge.

Education is the fundamental right of every student and our efforts clearly reflect this fact.

Come and join us and dive into the world of learning and spread the light of wisdom in all directions.
